Jill Lefkowitz

Jill Lefkowitz has been a makeup artist for over 30 years. She has worked with many celebrities and has worked on commericals, TV shows,
films and print. She recently worked on the film "Bad Boys For Life" and the TV show "The Marvelous Mrs. Maisel". She lives in South Florida with her husband and 15 year old son.
